🌷 About Orchid Junior

A Stepping Stone To A Lifetime Of Happy Learning!

"A child's life is like a piece of paper on which every passerby leaves a mark — but we leave an impression!" 🌸

Orchid Junior is an enabler, helping little ones grow into smart, self-motivated, "Ready for Life" superstars! Here, making a new friend matters just as much as learning to read — and singing a song is as wonderful as counting all the way to a hundred. We believe nature helps children grow in every way, so our whole curriculum is built to bring your child closer to the great outdoors.

❓ Good Questions

Everything You're Wondering About!

What's the minimum age for admission?

Our Buds toddler programme welcomes children from 1.5 years with a parent alongside. Petals playgroup begins at 2 years for independent attendance.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We keep a friendly 10:1 ratio so every child gets real, individual attention every single day.

Is transport available?

Yes! We offer air-conditioned transport covering Sarabha Nagar and nearby areas of Ludhiana.

What safety measures are in place?

CCTV cameras, a fireproof facility, child-safe flooring, controlled visitor access and constant teacher supervision.

How do I book a campus visit?

Fill in the form above, call us directly, or message us on WhatsApp — we'll set up a time that works for you!
